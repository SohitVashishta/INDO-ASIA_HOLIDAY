    Top Tourist Places and Attractions in SWITZERLAND

    Lindenhof Old Town

    Visit Lindenhof to get a perfect view of the Old Town, Grossmunster Church, City Hall, sparkling Limmat River, the University of Zurich and the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology. Lindenhof is a district and the oldest part of the city and once, a Roman Fort stood in its place on the hill. With an eventful history, even after the fall of the Roman Empire, Lindenhof always had an important role in the city’s history. Today, the lush Lindenhof in the heart of Zurich, is a must visit to get an incredible view of the city! Sit under a tree to admire the views or indulge yourself in a game of chess on a giant chessboard. ...Read More

       

    Mount Titlis Rotair

    Mount Titlis Rotair is world’s first revolving cableway, the first cable span carried travellers from the village of Engelberg to the summit of Mt Titlis in 1927 and hence the aerial ride was converted into one of the first cable cars in the Swiss Alps. Admire the dramatic alpine vistas of Mount Titlis. Taking the cable car to reach the summit of the mountain at above 10,000 feet is an exhilarating experience. The Rotair revolving cable car one of its kind offering 360-degrees panorama of the Swiss Alps. ...Read More

       

    Mount Pilatus Excursion

    A visit to Mount Pilatus is a perfect way to spend your day in Lucerne. Hop on the gondola ride or take the world’s steepest cogwheel train to get the vistas of the Alps. Overlooking the city of Lucerne, Mount Pilatus is a rugged mountain at 6,982 feet. Enjoy the views from a cosy restaurant or café at the summit or go hiking on the Dragon Trail. With a number of activities and stunning views, Mount Pilatus is a must visit spot in Switzerland. ...Read More

       

    Mount Pilatus Cogwheel Train

    Ride the world’s steepest cogwheel railways down through the rock faces, alluring mountain streams, and lush meadows to reach Alpnachstad. The spectacular train ride is the steepest in the world with fierce landscapes. Check out the many bars and restaurants or head to the terrace to enjoy the view. Explore the mythical “Dragon Path” around these mountains and hike to the vantage points to capture the moments.

       

    Matterhorn Peak

    The mystical and magical, Matterhorn Peak fascinates you the moment you lay eyes on it. The sharp-pyramid like Matterhorn is the pride of Zermatt, a beauty that beckons you to explore it. The Matterhorn glacier paradise is the highest summer ski region in Europe and accessible throughout the year. Take an unforgettable excursion to the top and admire the majestic Matterhorn from up close.

       

    Jungfraujoch Excursion

    Perched between Lakes Thun and Brienz, Interlaken is a small town offering majestic views of Jungfrau and Matterhorn. Take a trip to Jungfraujoch for a thrilling and unforgettable experience in Switzerland. The majestic Jungfraujoch is hard to miss with its view, magnificent backdrop of ice, snow and rock with stunning bird’s eye view of the valley. The sheer grandeur of the Swiss Alps, the alluring view of Grindelwald and the might Jungfraujoch Peak, a day trip to Jungfraujoch is a perfect day to spend your time in Interlaken. ...Read More

       

    Gornergrat Excursion

    Surrounded by lush valleys and high in the Valais Alps, Zermatt is one of the best places to admire the panoramic views of the Alps. Gornergrat Bahn is one of the must do rail excursions in Zermatt. The highest open-air rack railway in Europe, Gornergrat offers a view of 29 four thousand-metre peaks. A rail-ride to Gornergrat is one of the most popular attraction of Switzerland. Gape at the fantastic vistas of Matherhorn, the Breithorn, Monte Rosa (Europe's second-highest peak, at 4633m), Mont Blanc (Europe's highest peak at 4810 metres, found in Chamonix) and the Gornergletsher glacier. ...Read More

       

    Polybahn Funicular

    Ride on an aerial cable car to the peak of Mt Stanserhorn to get stunning views of the Swiss Alps. The traditional wooden funicular railway car is the best way to admire the stunning countryside and the cities of Switzerland. Take cable car was the first of its kind – open top aerial cable car in the world.

       

    Swiss Federal Institute of Technology

    Follow the footsteps of great researchers, scientist, and Nobel Prize winners and explore the majestic Swiss Federal Institute of Technology. Discover the art collection that portrays the hidden cultural gems o Switzerland.

       

    Zurich Opera House

    The outstanding acoustics and wide variety of international performances, Zurich Opera House is well-known and one of the best in the world. It was awarded in 2014 as the Best Opera Company of the Year at the International Opera Awards. The traditional structure is undoubtedly one of the must-visit spots for its unmatched beauty.

       

    Village of Engelberg

    Engelberg is a major mountain resort in Central Switzerland known for its 'house mountain' Titlis. In the Middle Ages, Engelberg was known for the educational quality of its Benedictine monastery, Engelberg Abbey. From the 19th Century onwards Engelberg became internationally known as a mountain resort, but it is today visited as much for skiing as for its Alpine character. With its combination of modern snow and sports facilities and alpine location, Engelberg is popular today for both summer and winter tourism. ...Read More

       

    Lake Lucerne Cruise

    Relax and take in the beauty of historical and natural treasures of Lucerne with Lake Lucerne Cruise. Lake Lucerne in central Switzerland is the fourth largest in the country with a scenic backdrop and fascinating vistas. Hop on a cruise over the majestic lake and experience a peaceful ride surrounded by mountains and quaint villages. To get the best of Lucerne, this exhilarating cruise adventure is a must do in Switzerland.

       

    Glacier Express

    Trip to Switzerland is incomplete without a panoramic trip on the Glacier Express that takes you through the scenic mountains in the one of the most leisurely way possible. It is one of the slowest express trains in the world and for a good reason. Glacier Express is a direct train from Zermatt to St. Moritz, take you through breath-taking vistas of Alps, narrow valleys, tunnels and across over 250 bridges. It surely is a must do things in Switzerland to have an incredible experience. ...Read More

       

    Lake Geneva Cruise

    Stretching from Geneva in the south to Villeneuve in the east, Lake Geneva is the largest lake in Switzerland. Make the most of the majestic scenery of Geneva and head for a magical cruise experience on Lake Geneva and explore the city’s iconic spots from a different view with the backdrop of the Alps.

       

    Mount Pilatus Gondola Ride

    Mount Pilatus tour is a must do when you are in Lucerne. The iconic spot of Lucerne, Mount Pilatus is easily accessible the Valley Station in Krienseregg. Experience the fun gondola ride of 30-minutes to admire the spectacular vistas over the lakes and the Alps. The incredible ride will make you fall in love with Switzerland’s mountain sceneries.

       

    Mount Titlis Panorama

    The picture-perfect Engelberg Village at the foot of north face of Mount Titlis is the gateway to the majestic mountains. Take the Rotair Cable Car to reach the top of Mount Titlis and admire the 360-degrees panorama of the Swiss Alps. Enjoy the visit to glittering Ice Gotto or take a ride with the Ice Flyer which bring you up close to the glacier’s deep crevasses and gigantic ice boulders. Witness the beauty of the alluring views of the Alps from the restaurants or the sun terrace. ...Read More
