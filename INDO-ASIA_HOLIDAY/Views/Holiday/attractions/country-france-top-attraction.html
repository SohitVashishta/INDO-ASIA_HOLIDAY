    Top Tourist Places and Attractions in FRANCE

    Level 2 Eiffel Tower

    Treat your eyes with the splendour that Paris is from level 2 of the grandeur Eiffel Tower. Enjoy the stunning vistas of monuments of French’s capital The Louvre, Notre Dame, Grand Palais, the gorgeous Seine River and a bird’s eye view of the enchanting city.

       

    Seine River Cruise

    A ride on the shimmering Seine River through the heart of the city is the best way to explore the marvellous monuments of Paris. Relax and take in the beauty of the perfect scenic setting of Paris and its stunning architectural marvels. Seine River Cruise is one of the must-do things in Paris to get the best of France. To see the city in a different version, hop on the cruise at night and witness the ever-beautiful Paris illuminated at its best.

       

    Place de la Concorde

    The splendid Place de la Concorde is the largest square in Paris. Situated between the Tuileries and Champs-Elysees, Place de la Concorde is one of the must visit spots in Paris. The square is well-known for the Luxor Obelisk, a 3,300-year-old Egyptian obelisk, prominent hotels and two fountains, Fontaine des Mers and Fontaine des Fleuves. The intriguing history of Place de la Concorde attracts travellers from around the globe. Once created as an execution site during the French Revolution, the square is now one of the most fascinating sights in Paris. ...Read More

       

    Louvre Museum - Illumination Tour

    Explore the mystical Louvre Museum at night, one of the must-visit spots in Paris. Exhibition spaces and the museum are closed at night, but the crowd is much less and lights on this the museum becomes somehow even more picturesque than during the daytime.

       

    Louvre Museum Inside Tour

    One of the largest museums in the world, Louvre is well-known for its amazing art collection and masterpieces by legendary artists: The Mona Lisa by Leonardo Da Vinci, Venus de Milo by Rembrandt, Vermeer, Caravaggio and many more. Home to over 35,000 splendid collections of paintings and sculptures, the Louvre is regarded as one of the greatest museums in the world. Nestled centrally, along the right bank of Seine River, Louvre Museum is undoubtedly one of the must-visit spots in Paris. ...Read More

       

    Eiffel Tower - Illumination Tour

    Experience Paris by night with the illumination tour of Eiffel Tower. One of the most visited spots in Europe, Eiffel Tower looks absolutely stunning when it’s lit at its best at night. The iconic tower defines Paris and sets an example of brilliant architectural skills. Witness the amazing light show and the history of this grandeur. Admire the gleaming iron structure bursts into mesmerizing sparkles.

       

    Disneyland Paris

    Experience the Magic of Disneyland in Paris! A world where make-believe exists and a wonderful world of Disney comes to life. The resort has two parks, Disneyland Park with fun rides, restaurants, cafes and numerous shop to indulge in and Walt Disney Studios for the Disney movies buff with intriguing movie rides and stage-sets. Admire the picture-perfect Adventureland, stroll around the Main Street USA, or greet your favourite character from Disney at Fantasyland. ...Read More

       

    Arc de Triomphe

    Overlooking Champs-Elysees, Arc de Triomphe is one of the most iconic monuments in the world. A tribute by Napoleon to his Grande armée, the beautiful structure was built in 1806 to honour those who fought and died in the French Revolution and Napoleonic Wars. Regarded as one of the most beautiful of all the triumphal arches, Arc de Triomphe is a must-visit monument in Paris.
