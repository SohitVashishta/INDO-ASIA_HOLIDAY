    A country that very much lives by its Spanish word Fiesta because life itself is a fiesta here. As the birthplace of Antoni Gaudi, Pablo Picasso and Manolo Blahnik, Spain have world-class art museums, palaces, and cathedrals. The overwhelming architecture, the tap-tap of Flamenco, ancient Roman ruins, soaring mountain peaks and the irresistible cuisine make Spain one of the major travel destinations in Europe.

    To find the soul of Spain, visit Madrid, a city that comes to life at midnight. The capital city of Spain, Madrid is loved for its nightlife. Take a look at the glorious Royal Palace, explore the world-class art collection at one of the most famous museums in the world at Prado Museum. Praise the beauty of Baroque style bridge of Madrid. Toledo Bridge is at its best when its illuminated by lights at night. Stroll around Puerta Del Sol, one of the most famous squares in Madrid.

    Barcelona has its own unique charm, not the capital city but almost feels like it with its iconic attractions. Home to the architectural marvels and sunny beaches, Barcelona is most loved amongst travelers. Antoni Gaudis most symbolic project, Sagrada Familia is a sight to watch. Still under construction from 1882, this marvel is definitely worth a visit. One of the finest examples of Gothic architecture, Barcelona Cathedral is a sight to behold. Barcelona is incomplete without a visit to the strange but enchanting Park Guell, again one of the intriguing structures by Antoni Gaudi.

    Bustling with culture and music, the vibrant city of Seville is best known for its delicious cuisine, Moorish castle of Alcazar, and the fascinating Flamenco Dance. Enjoy the vibrancy of Valencia, one of the most loved city in Europe nestled by the Mediterranean Sea. The stunning landscapes and picture-perfect beaches of Valencia make it one of the most popular destinations in Spain. Head to Plaza de la Reina, City of Arts and Science, Plaza de Toros and Torres de Serrano to get the best of the city!
