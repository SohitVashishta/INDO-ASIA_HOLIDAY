    KARNATAKA Travel Guide

    A stunning state of South India, Karnataka is a compelling blend of ancient ruins, exceptional cities, splendid palaces, quaint hill stations and glittering beaches. Dipped in rich culture and heritage, from picturesque landscapes to delicious local cuisine and fine art, Karnataka has it all. A trip to Karnataka is vacation amidst hundreds of years' worth of history. It is an ultimate experience for the travellers to view the majestic monuments that range from gigantic forts, artistic mausoleums, bustling cities to the temples.


    The beauty of Karnataka isn't just in its art and history but also religious ethnicity. The best places to get a glimpse of the state's religious side are Murudeshwar, Mysore, and Hassan. The beautiful state is blessed with alluring hill stations and its lush rolling mountains. Coorg is one of the top destinations in Karnataka for its coffee plantations and picture-perfect vistas. Chikmagalur, Ooty and Kodaikanal are a few other places that are adored for verdant hills and breathtaking views.

    A stunning state of South India, Karnataka is a compelling blend of ancient ruins, exceptional cities, splendid palaces, quaint hill stations and glittering beaches. Dipped in rich culture and heritage, from picturesque landscapes to delicious local cuisine and fine art, Karnataka has it all. A trip to Karnataka is vacation amidst hundreds of years' worth of history. It is an ultimate experience for the travellers to view the majestic monuments that range from gigantic forts, artistic mausoleums, bustling cities to the temples.


    The beauty of Karnataka isn't just in its art and history but also religious ethnicity. Blended with their long histories, the religious heritage of the state has transformed into various sacred sites. The best places to get a glimpse of the state's religious side are Murudeshwar, Mysore, and Hassan. The beautiful state is blessed with alluring hill stations and its lush rolling mountains. Coorg is one of the top destinations in Karnataka for its coffee plantations and picture-perfect vistas. Chikmagalur, Ooty and Kodaikanal are a few other places that are adored for verdant hills and breathtaking views.


    The cities of Karnataka are as spectacular as its hill stations. Mysore has an extraordinary royal heritage, and Mysore Palace is a must-visit tourist attraction in the city. The capital city of Karnataka, Bangalore is home to India's IT industry. The city is loved for its greenery, enthralling buildings and temples. One of India's top historical destinations, the quaint village of Hampi was once the last capital of Vijayanagar, one of the greatest Hindu empires in India's history. The beautiful temples and 14th-century ruins are a sight to behold. Before you head out of the state, take a stroll along Gokarna Beach, one of the well-known beaches of South India!
