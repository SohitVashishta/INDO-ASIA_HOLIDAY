    SWEDEN Travel Guide

    The vibrant coastlines, beautiful lakes, lush forests and snow-capped mountains define the beauty of Sweden. Along with the splendid natural beauty, this stunning country has a long and interesting history that will leave in awe. Made up of up of thousands of islands, deep pine, rolling hills and golden beaches, Sweden is a land of contrast. With great outdoors, Sweden is an awesome place to spend your vacation. Scandinavia’s heart, Sweden is a perfect place to relax and unwind. Aesthetic architecture, Morden art blended with cobblestoned streets of the old town, the capital city of Stockholm offers a mind-blowing experience. Sweden’s beauty is defined by its capital city, Stockholm. Explore the royalty and luxury of the Royal Palace, it is also regarded as one of the largest palaces in Europe. Don’t miss to explore the iconic buildings of Stockholm, such as Parliament Building, Stockholm City Hall and take a peek into Vasa Museum to indulge in the history of the country.
