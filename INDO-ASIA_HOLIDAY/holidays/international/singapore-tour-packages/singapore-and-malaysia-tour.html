    Arrive at Singapore

    Day 2 - Singapore < >

    Singapore City Tour | Night Safari

    After breakfast, head for a half-day city tour. Visit Merlion Park, Orchard Road and China Town. Later, enjoy the Night Safari tour.

    Overnight at Singapore. 

    Chinatown

    A perfect blend of traditional and modern, Chinatown is a cultural treasure of Singapore. Explore the Chinese heritage of the famous Chinatown in Singapore with its vibrant shops and amazing restaurants and café. Find moments of peace and tranquillity at Buddha Tooth Relic Temple and Museum as you explore its alluring rooftop garden. The bustling Chinatown is a paradise for food lovers. It will leave you in awe with its authentic cuisine and distinct dishes. The colourful shops, historical architectures and the delicious street food make Chinatown one of the must-visit spots in Singapore.

    Merlion Park

    Merlion Park is a personification of Singapore. The iconic statues, nestled in the Merlion Park, of a beautiful mythical creature with the head of a lion and body of fish, is a major attraction in Singapore. The sculptures represent Singapore's modest beginning as a fishing village, when it was called Temasek, meaning 'sea town' in Old Javanese. Overlooking the Marina Bay, Merlion Park is one of the must-visit spots in Singapore

    Night Safari

    Singapore night safari is the first of its kind in the world. Pioneer among the nocturnal zoos, it’s an exhilarating experience to venture into the world of animals during the night. 12-time winner of Best Attraction category awarded by Singapore Tourism, Night Safari in Singapore is one the must-do things! With over 2,500 resident animals from over 130 species, including many indigenous to the region, such as the endangered Asian elephant, Malayan tapir and Malayan tiger, the Night Safari is a thrilling experience. Explore the alluring tropical jungle on foot, and wander through the many trails of that lets you see many animals not visible from the tram. Stay up and little late to take this adventure of a lifetime.

    Orchard Road

    Singapore’s iconic shopping strip, Orchard Road is lined with glitzy mall like Ion Orchard to the retro maze of Lucky Plaza. The swanky street with bright trees and filled with distinctive shopping stalls, Orchard Road is Singapore’s favourite place and popular throughout the world. With plentiful cafes, fine dining selections and restaurants, Orchard Road is one of the famous tourist spots in Singapore.

    < >

    Day 3 - Singapore < >

    Sentosa Island Excursion

    After breakfast, spend the morning at leisure. Later, visit Sentosa Island. Ride the cable car from Moun Faber to the island and enjoy stunning panoramic views of Sentose Merlion and Tiger Sky Tower. Enjoy the skyline luge ride - a must for all age groups! Visit the S.E.A. Aquarium and end your day with the magical Wings of Time show.

    Overnight at Singapore. 

    S.E.A. Aquarium

    S.E.A Aquarium on Sentosa Island is one of Singapore’s exciting family-friendly and all-weather attractions. It is renowned as one of the largest aquariums in the world and is home to over 100,000 aquatic animals from over 800 species across 49 habitats. There are creatures from all the world’s major oceans, with exhibits specially designed to recreate the conditions of their natural habitats. A must visit place on Sentosa Island, your journey into the breathtaking underwater realm of fascinating sea creatures will be an unforgettable one.

    Skyline Luge

    To get the panoramic vistas of Sentosa Island, board the Skyline Luge! It is perfect spot for families looking for a fun and thrilling day in Sentosa Island. This exciting attraction is suitable for all ages! Take the adventure on wheeled cart ride and enjoy a picture-perfect view of the island.

    Wings of Time

    Sentosa’s iconic night show, Wings of Time tells a tale of friendship, courage, mystery and magic that will thrill you till the end. Explore the alluring display of water, laser, and fire as it creates a breath-taking vistas with a musical background. Journey with Shahbaz, the mythical bird-like creature and his human friends and enjoy the spectacular show.

    Day 5 - Kuala Lumpur < >

    Kuala Lumpur City Tour

    After breakfast, leave for a half-day city tour. Visit Petronas Twin Towers (photostop), National Palace, Independence Square and National Monument.

    Overnight at Kuala Lumpur.

    National Monument

    The National Monument is a beautiful sculpture built in the remembrance of the brave soldiers who died fighting for the independence of the country. Located around the Lake Gardens, the National Monument is a must visit in Kuala Lumpur.

    National Palace

    Nestled around the breath-taking landscapes and verdant nature, the National Palace or the Jalan Istana of Malaysia is one of the most admirable sights. Open to all, National Palace has a separate part inside where the Royal Museum exhibits the paintings of national artist. The simple yet elegant palace is a must-visit place in Kuala Lumpur.

    Petronas Twin Towers

    World’s famous and architectural marvels, Petronas Twin Towers is the crown of Kuala Lumpur, nestled in the heart of the city. The tallest twin towers in the world will amaze you with its magnificent structures and amazing architecture. The breath-taking skyline from the towers is one of the must-see views in Malaysia. The fascinating towers are linked through a sky bridge at the 41st and 42nd floor to allow people to travel within the tower. The iconic 88 storey chrome and steel towers are one of the must-visit spots in Malaysia.
